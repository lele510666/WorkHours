# WorkHours Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

WorkHours respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how WorkHours handles user data on supported platforms, including iOS and Android.

WorkHours is a time tracking app designed to help users record work hours, manage projects, calculate earnings, export reports, and optionally use automatic clock-in features.

---

## Data Collection

WorkHours does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our own servers.

Specifically:

- No account registration is required
- No login is required
- No analytics or tracking services are used
- No advertising SDKs are used
- No personal data is sold or shared
- No work records are uploaded to external servers operated by us

---

## Local Data Storage

All data created in WorkHours is stored locally on your device.

This include:

- work hour records
- project information
- hourly rates
- notes and memos
- app preferences
- export settings
- automatic clock settings

Your data remains under your control.

---

## iCloud Sync (iOS Only)

If iCloud Sync is enabled on iOS, your data may be securely synchronized through Apple's iCloud service.

WorkHours does not access, store, or process this cloud data outside Apple's infrastructure.

---

## Location Permissions

WorkHours may request location access to support optional location-based features.

These features include:

- selecting a work location
- automatic clock-in reminders
- automatic clock-in / clock-out
- detecting arrival at or departure from saved work locations

If you enable automatic clock features, WorkHours may use foreground or background location access to detect relevant location changes.

Important:

- location access is optional
- automatic clock features are optional
- location processing happens locally on your device
- location data is not uploaded to our servers
- location data is not shared with third parties
- you can disable location access at any time in your device settings

---

## File Export

If you choose to export reports (such as CSV files), WorkHours will create files locally on your device.

These files are only generated when you explicitly request export.

---

## Permissions We Do Not Use

WorkHours does not access:

- contacts
- photos
- microphone
- camera
- messages
- personal identifiers

unless clearly required by future features and disclosed in an updated policy.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future if WorkHours adds new features or changes how data is handled.

Any updates will be reflected on this page.
